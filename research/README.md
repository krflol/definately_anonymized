# Emerging Treatment Research

This folder tracks science-backed but new or trial-stage treatment areas relevant to the current working diagnosis:

- metastatic p16-positive squamous cell carcinoma in a patient-right cervical lymph node;
- probable HPV-mediated head-and-neck primary, with primary site and stage still pending.

## Maturity Map

| Area | Current maturity for this profile | Practical meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Standard staging and curative-intent treatment | Standard | Do not delay this to chase experimental therapy. |
| PD-1 checkpoint immunotherapy | Approved in defined HNSCC settings | Important to discuss; not automatically a substitute for surgery/radiation/chemoradiation. |
| Therapeutic HPV vaccines and mRNA vaccines | Clinical trial | Most relevant if HPV genotype, especially HPV16, is confirmed. |
| HPV-targeted TCR-T / engineered cell therapy | Clinical trial | Often HLA-restricted and usually recurrent/metastatic setting. |
| CRISPR/direct HPV gene editing | Preclinical to very early translational | Track scientifically; not a current standard treatment path. |
| HPV ctDNA / TTMV-HPV DNA | Emerging clinical tool and clinical trials | Potential baseline/surveillance/risk-adaptation role. |
| Deintensification | Trial or carefully selected use | Should be tumor-board or protocol driven. |

## Highest-Yield Missing Data

- PET/CT and full staging.
- Primary-site identification.
- HPV DNA/RNA and genotype, if ordered.
- PD-L1 CPS.
- Resectability.
- Treatment intent.
- Trial eligibility and referral path.
