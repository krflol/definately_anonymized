# Therapeutic HPV Vaccines And mRNA

## Bottom Line

Therapeutic HPV vaccines are one of the most relevant emerging-treatment categories for a p16-positive SCC profile. These are not preventive HPV vaccines; they are designed to help the immune system attack cancer cells expressing HPV antigens such as E6 and E7.

Most are clinical-trial therapies.

## Why HPV16 Matters

Many trials target HPV16. p16 positivity supports HPV-mediated disease but does not prove HPV16. HPV genotype can determine whether a trial applies.

## Ask Oncology

- Can the tumor be tested for HPV genotype?
- Are any therapeutic HPV vaccine trials appropriate before standard treatment?
- If no trial applies now, when should the search be repeated?
- Would referral to a high-volume head-and-neck cancer center or trial center be appropriate?
